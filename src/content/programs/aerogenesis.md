## 12-Week Aviation Tutoring Course

Aerogenesis is not just a course; it is an initiation into the world of aviation. Designed specifically for Ethiopian youth, this program breaks down the complex science of flight into digestible, exciting modules.

### Course Syllabus

**Week 1 – Introduction to Aviation & History**
*   First flights, pioneers of aviation.
*   Evolution of airplanes.
*   Fun aviation facts and global context.

**Week 2 – Aircraft Design & Systems**
*   How airplanes are built.
*   Wings, engines, and control surfaces.
*   Simple aerodynamics (Lift, Drag, Thrust, Weight).

**Week 3 – Flight Operations**
*   Takeoff, cruising, and landing mechanics.
*   Flight planning basics.
*   Basic pilot workflow and cockpit management.

**Week 4 – Air Traffic Control (ATC)**
*   Communication protocols and radio discipline.
*   Radar and flight paths.
*   Safety coordination between pilots and controllers.

**Week 5 – Aviation Safety & Problem-Solving**
*   Checklists and safety procedures.
*   Real-life scenario problem-solving activities.
*   The "Swiss Cheese Model" of accident causation.

**Week 6 – Aviation Regulations & Rules of the Sky**
*   Understanding ICAO, FAA, ECAA, and IATA.
*   Why rules exist and how they keep us safe.
*   Classroom group activities on regulation.

**Week 7 – Aviation Management: Airlines & Airports**
*   How airlines and airports are organized.
*   Teamwork, ground operations, and logistics.
*   The business side of flying.

**Week 8 – Aviation Technology**
*   Jet engines and propulsion.
*   Autopilot, avionics, GPS, and radar.
*   The Black Box (FDR/CVR) and future innovations.

**Week 9 – Aviation Careers**
*   Roles: Pilots, engineers, air traffic controllers.
*   Cabin crew, airport management, and futuristic roles.
*   Pathways to entry.

**Week 10 – Aviation Industry Trends & Future of Flight**
*   Green aviation and sustainability.
*   AI, drones, and supersonic jets.
*   Urban air mobility and space tourism.

**Week 11 – Aviation Career Pathways & Personal Dreams**
*   Creating a personal aviation dream plan.
*   Exploring specific career paths in Ethiopia.
*   Preparing for the future.

**Week 12 – Wrap-Up, Showcase & Graduation**
*   Review of all 11 weeks.
*   Project showcase and reflection activity.
*   Certificates and inspiration for the next step.

> "Aviation is proof that given the will, we have the capacity to achieve the impossible."
