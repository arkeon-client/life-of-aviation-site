## 12-Week Aviation Tutoring Course

Aerogenesis is not just a course; it is an initiation into the world of aviation. Designed specifically for Ethiopian youth, this program breaks down the complex science of flight into digestible, exciting modules.

### Program Overview

This comprehensive 12-week journey covers everything from the **History of Flight** to advanced **Future Technologies**.

**What You Will Learn:**
*   **Flight Fundamentals:** Aerodynamics, Engines, and Systems.
*   **Operations:** ATC Communication, Safety, and Regulations (ICAO/FAA).
*   **Career Pathways:** How to become a Pilot, Engineer, or Manager.
*   **The Future:** Green Aviation, AI, and Space Tourism.

**Secure Your Seat:**
The full detailed syllabus and course materials are available exclusively to enrolled cadets in the Flight Deck dashboard.
