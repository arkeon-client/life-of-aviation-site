## Navigating Your Career

The path to becoming a pilot or aviation professional is rarely a straight line. It is filled with financial hurdles, academic challenges, and tough decisions. Our Mentorship program provides you with a co-pilot for your career.

### What We Offer

*   **One-on-One Guidance**: Monthly calls to track your progress and set goals.
*   **Airline Prep**: Mock interviews designed to mimic the selection process of major airlines (like Ethiopian Airlines).
*   **CV & Resume Polish**: We help you highlight the skills that aviation recruiters are looking for.

### Who is this for?
This is for the aspiring pilot who has the passion but needs the roadmap. If you are applying to flight school, currently in training, or looking for your first job, this mentorship is for you.
